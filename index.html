A New Vision for Nyabasi West

Hon. Gisiri Maroa understands the power of devolution and the needs of Nyabasi West.
As a champion of grassroots leadership, I believe in bringing resources closer to the people, ensuring every ward gets its fair share, and empowering every family, youth, and farmer.
Together, we will harness the promise of devolution to deliver real solutions: better roads, quality healthcare, modern agriculture, and opportunities for all.
Your vote is your voice. Register, participate, and let's build a future of dignity and progress for Nyabasi West!

Your Voice in Government

As a lifelong resident of Nyabasi West, I understand the challenges our community faces. My experience in community organizing and public service has prepared me to be your effective representative.

I'm running for office because I believe Nyabasi West deserves leadership that puts people first. My campaign is built on three pillars: transparency, accountability, and progress.

With your support in 2027, we'll work together to improve infrastructure, create economic opportunities, and ensure our ward gets its fair share of resources.

Our Action Plan for Nyabasi West

🏗️

Infrastructure Development

Prioritize road repairs, clean water access, and electricity upgrades across all zones of Nyabasi West.

👩‍🎓

Education & Youth

Invest in school facilities, vocational training programs, and youth employment initiatives.

🏥

Healthcare Access

Expand clinic services and implement mobile health units to reach underserved areas.

💼

Economic Growth

Support local businesses with micro-loans and create markets for agricultural products.

🌳

Environmental Protection

Implement waste management systems and protect our natural resources.

🏛️

Transparent Governance

Quarterly town halls and public budget reports to ensure accountability.
