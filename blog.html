Our Vision for Nyabasi West
Imagine a ward where every family thrives, every youth is empowered, and every farmer prospers.

Hon. Gisiri Maroa is committed to leading Nyabasi West into a new era of opportunity, dignity, and sustainable growth.
  • Championing poverty eradication through innovative programs and partnerships.
  • Transforming agriculture with modern techniques, market access, and value addition.
  • Engaging every voter with scientific, data-driven strategies for inclusive development.
  • Building a transparent, accountable, and people-centered leadership for all.
Our Campaign Pillars
Change That Matters
We believe in real, measurable change. From better roads to quality healthcare, our agenda is action, not promises.
Poverty Eradication
No family should go hungry. We will launch targeted social programs, youth empowerment, and microfinance initiatives to lift every household.
Agricultural Revolution
Agriculture is our backbone. We will support farmers with training, inputs, irrigation, and market linkages for higher yields and incomes.
