Our Vision for Nyabasi West

Hon. Gisiri Maroa is committed to leading Nyabasi West into a new era of opportunity, dignity, and sustainable growth. With a background in community development and a passion for service, Hon. Maroa brings fresh energy and innovative solutions to our ward's challenges.

Our campaign is built on four foundational pillars that address the most pressing needs of our community while laying the groundwork for long-term prosperity.

  • Championing poverty eradication through innovative programs
  • Transforming agriculture with modern techniques
  • Engaging every voter with data-driven strategies
  • Building transparent, accountable leadership
Learn About Our Pillars

Our Campaign Pillars

These four pillars form the foundation of our vision for Nyabasi West

Change That Matters

We believe in real, measurable change. From better roads to quality healthcare, our agenda is action, not promises.

Poverty Eradication

No family should go hungry. We will launch targeted social programs, youth empowerment, and microfinance initiatives.

Agricultural Revolution

Agriculture is our backbone. We will support farmers with training, inputs, irrigation, and market linkages.

Voter Engagement

Your voice matters! We use scientific analysis and digital tools to listen, learn, and act on your needs.

Engage. Participate. Decide.

We want to hear from you! Share your views, ideas, and priorities for Nyabasi West.

  • Take part in our community surveys and scientific polls
  • Join our WhatsApp groups and digital townhalls
  • Volunteer for door-to-door campaigns
  • Follow us on social media for updates
